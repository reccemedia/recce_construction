About Us

Sunraise Polymers

Sunraise Polymers was created in 2007 to trade various raw materials to the Polymer Industries mainly Wovensack Industries, PE Film Manufacturers, Plastics Injection moulders, Rotomoulded Parts, Box Strapping Industries, Leno Bags Manufacturers, White Goods parts moulders, Drip Irrigation Pipe manufacturers, Plastics Blow moulders etc.

In 2008, Sunraise Polymers founds the Konkan Speciality Polyproducts Private Limited [KONSPEC] Masterbatch division as their Strategic partner to service the various targeted Petrochemical segments by Sunraise Polymers. Today, Sunraise Polymers started servicing their Domestic Indian Customers and also exports KONSPEC Masterbatch in more than 15 countries.
